WEATHERSTRIPS

These products keep out rain, winds and noise

These products are attached to doors and window frames to protect the cabin from wind, rain and noise. They also serve many other roles, including smooth opening and closing of doors and raising and lowering of windows. Toyoda Gosei boasts a top share in the world market for these products, which are provided globally from 16 production sites worldwide.

Luggage Weatherstrips

These are rubber products affixed to the body side of the trunk, protecting the trunk interior from the elements. They also absorb impact when the trunk is opened and closed and prevent backlash and rattle.
