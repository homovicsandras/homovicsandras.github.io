Here at Toyoda Gosei UK we pride ourselves on being specialists in the manufacturing and production of first class rubber weather-strips.

Our vision and goal is to be a world class supplier in the fast paced and competitive automotive industry. Our people are dedicated to producing quality parts with a right first time approach in order to keep our customers satisfied.

Established in 1999 we have grown and developed as an organisation and are always seeking opportunity to improve and develop both process and our people. What we do is very important to us but equally as important is how we do it, the TG values and behaviours guide us in how we act towards each other as to how we treat our customers.

The TG values are set in place to help us achieve this together.

  • Customer Oriented
  • Challenge
  • Ownership
  • Continuous Kaizen
  • Respect
  • Teamwork
